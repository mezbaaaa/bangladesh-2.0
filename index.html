Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on fostering entrepreneurship, technology advancements, and sustainable industries to create a dynamic, future-ready economy.

Sustainability

Addressing climate change, renewable energy, and green infrastructure builds a more sustainable, environmentally conscious nation for future generations.

Infrastructure

Expanding broadband access, 5G networks, and tech hubs strengthens the foundation for a thriving digital economy and innovation.

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on gender equality, youth skills, community participation, and financial inclusion, fostering innovation, equity, and sustainable growth.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dr. Mohammad Yunus, a Nobel Peace Prize laureate, is renowned for pioneering microfinance and social business models. His vision for the future focuses on eradicating poverty by empowering individuals through financial inclusion, especially women and marginalized communities. He believes in creating sustainable businesses that prioritize social impact over profit. Yunus envisions a world where economic systems foster equality, education, and environmental sustainability, ensuring prosperity and dignity for all, particularly those excluded from traditional financial systems.
